# Sawangan

## Location
- South Depok sub-district, adjacent to Cinere
- Borders: Cinere (west), Pancoran Mas (north)

## Infrastructure
- Shopping: Margo City Mall, Sawangan Residence commercial area
- Education: Several private schools and universities
- Connectivity: Close to Jagorawi toll exit

## Area Characteristics
- Emerging middle-upper residential area
- Mix of landed houses and apartments
- Popular for young families and professionals
- Good balance of price and amenities
