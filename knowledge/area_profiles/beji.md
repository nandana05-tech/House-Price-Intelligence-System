# Beji

## Location
- Central sub-district in Depok, West Java
- Borders: Depok city center, UI area to the north

## Infrastructure
- University access: Universitas Indonesia (UI) campus nearby
- Commercial: Depok Town Square, various local markets
- Public transport: Good KRL Commuter Line access via UI station

## Area Characteristics
- Student housing dominant due to UI proximity
- Mix of apartments, boarding houses (kost), and family homes
- High rental demand, moderate purchase prices
- Dense urban residential with good walkability
