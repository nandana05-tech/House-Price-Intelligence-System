# Property Pricing Factors in Depok

## Key Price Drivers
- **Location Premium**: Cinere > Sawangan > Beji (toll access, mall proximity)
- **Size Multiplier**: Larger LT/LB correlates with higher segment
- **Bed/Bath Ratio**: 3+ BR or 2+ BA indicates upper segment
- **Garage**: 2+ cars = premium family home
